About Me

Dedicated to student growth through movement, discipline, and teamwork

I am a passionate and dedicated Sports Teacher currently working at Mount Carmel College. My mission is to promote physical fitness, confidence, discipline, and teamwork among students while helping them develop a lifelong appreciation for sports and healthy living. I strongly believe that sports are essential for building character, leadership, resilience, and a positive mindset.

I value inclusivity, fairness, and sportsmanship, and I strive to create a safe and encouraging environment where every student—especially young girls—feels confident to participate and grow. Through structured training, fitness activities, and team-based learning, I aim to nurture well-rounded individuals who carry the values of discipline, respect, and perseverance both on and off the field.

Areas of Expertise

Curriculum-aligned sports education and student development

Training Minds. Strengthening Bodies. Building Leaders.

Through structured sports education and disciplined training, I focus on developing not only physical strength but also mental toughness, confidence, and leadership qualities in students. Sports become a foundation for success in academics and life.

Inspiring Excellence On and Off the Field

I guide students to strive for excellence by nurturing skill, sportsmanship, and a positive competitive spirit. My approach emphasizes fair play, teamwork, and resilience, helping students grow into responsible and confident individuals.

Empowering Students Through Sports & Wellness

By promoting fitness, wellness, and healthy habits, I empower students to take ownership of their physical and mental well-being. My goal is to create a safe, inclusive environment where every student feels motivated to participate and excel.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe physical education is essential for the holistic development of every child. My approach focuses on nurturing students’ physical fitness, confidence, and emotional well-being through inclusive, age-appropriate, and structured sports activities. I strive to create a safe and encouraging environment where every student feels valued and motivated to participate. By emphasizing individual growth, teamwork, discipline, and fair play, I help students develop healthy habits, self-belief, and resilience that support both academic success and lifelong well-being.
